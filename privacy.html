Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Re-Run App and visit our website.

Information We Collect

App Usage Data

  • Running session data (distance, time, speed, heart rate if connected)
  • Treadmill connection and performance data
  • App usage statistics and crash reports

Website Analytics

  • Basic website usage statistics through standard web analytics
  • No personally identifiable information is collected through the website

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

  • All running data is stored locally on your device by default
  • You maintain full control over your personal running data

Third-Party Integrations

If you choose to connect with third-party services:

  • Strava: Data is shared according to your Strava account settings and their privacy policy
  • You can disconnect third-party services at any time

Analytics and Improvement

  • Anonymous usage data helps us improve the app
  • No personal information is shared with third parties for analytics

Data Security

Local Data Protection

  • Your running data is stored securely on your local device
  • We recommend regular device backups to prevent data loss

No Server Storage

  • We do not store your personal running data on our servers
  • All data processing happens locally on your device

Your Rights

Data Control

  • You own all your running data
  • You can export, delete, or transfer your data at any time
  • No account registration is required to use the app

Third-Party Connections

  • You can connect or disconnect from services like Strava at any time
  • Third-party data sharing is entirely optional

Cookies and Tracking

Website

  • This website uses minimal analytics cookies
  • No tracking cookies for advertising purposes
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

Mobile App

  • The app does not use tracking cookies
  • Only functional data storage for app operation

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our About page.
