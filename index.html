The free open-source cross-platform Companion App for your Treadmill

Unleash your indoor run training, without needing any subscription.

Get Started

Look around, feel the track, experience the climbs!

The Re-Run app make it possible to relive the runs you did before on your treadmill by using your own recorded videos and gpx tracks.

Build your own unique library of memories!

The more you run, the more memories you will build. Memories that can be captured with your action cam. Scroll through the list and pick the run you want to relive again.

Give every route its own personality.

Set your preferences and tweak your upcoming run, decide if your treadmill should change its speed depending on the climb. Skip the first part of the run. It's all up to you.

Forget that you are running indoor!

In the augmented experience, your video will be enriched with progress information, while the treadmill responds to the terrain. The height meters are for real!

Show your progress to your friends!

At the end of every run, a summary is shown with all relevant statistics. If you want you can share the run details with the Strava community.

The Re-Run app was born out of frustration. In order to unlock the full potential of my treadmill, I had to get an expensive subscription to access my own running data. I would rather spend that amount of money yearly on running shoes.

Bas van de Sande - Running Enthusiast.
