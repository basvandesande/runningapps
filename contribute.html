This app started as a wild idea and quickly turned into a full-blown passion project. It’s open source, built with .NET MAUI, and already running on Windows and Android. But I want it everywhere — iOS, macOS, maybe even on a smart fridge someday 😄

Here’s the catch: I don’t have the Apple gear (or the budget) to tackle that side of things. So if you’re a dev with Apple experience — or just curious to explore — your help would be awesome.

Whether you want to fork the code, squash a bug, add a feature, or just bark some ideas my way, you’re more than welcome. The GitHub repo is open, the vibe is chill, and every contribution counts — even the tiny ones.

Let’s make this app smarter, faster, and maybe even more over the top. No gatekeeping. No paywalls. Just runners helping runners. Coders helping coders. And last but not least, a hyperactive beagle supervising the progress.
