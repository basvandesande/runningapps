--- title: 🏁 GET STARTED description: READY TO RUN SMARTER? LET'S GET YOU SET UP IN NO TIME. ---

Here’s what you need to hit the ground running (literally).

🧩 General Requirements

  • Bluetooth-Enabled Treadmill
    Your treadmill needs to speak Bluetooth LE — otherwise, it’s just a fancy clothes rack. The app connects via Bluetooth to track your runs and sync your data.

💻 Windows Setup

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or later
  • Processor: At least 1.6 GHz
  • Memory (RAM): Minimum 4GB (8GB recommended)
  • Storage: At least 500MB of free space

👉 Download from the Microsoft Store

📱 Android Setup

  • Android Version: Android 15.0 or later
  • Device: A tablet with Bluetooth (phones might work too)
  • Memory (RAM): Minimum 2GB (4GB recommended)
  • Storage: At least 500MB of free space

👉 Get it on Google Play

🏃‍♂️ That’s It!

Once installed, pair your treadmill, hit start, and let the app do its thing. No subscriptions. No paywalls. Just you, your data, and the open road (or belt).
