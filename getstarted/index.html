Prerequisites

>
    Bluetooth-Enabled Treadmill>
    The application requires a treadmill with Bluetooth functionality to connect and track your activity.
  • Bluetooth-Enabled Heart Rate Sensor>
    Optionally you can use youe Bluetooth heart rate sensor to get accurate heart rate data
> ## System Requirements ### Windows - **Operating System**: Windows 10 or later. - **Processor**: A modern processor with at least 1.6 GHz speed. - **Memory (RAM)**: Minimum 4GB (8GB recommended for better performance). - **Storage**: At least 500MB of free disk space. ### Android - **Android Version**: Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later. - **Device Requirements**: - A smartphone or tablet with Bluetooth capability. - Minimum 2GB of RAM (4GB recommended for smoother performance). - At least 100MB of free storage space. --- ## Additional Notes - The application integrates with **Strava**, so an internet connection may be required for certain features. - Ensure your device's date and time settings are accurate for proper functionality. - For the best experience, keep your device updated with the latest software and security patches. --- If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact the support team.
